Trix

Trix is one of the main characters in our story and is definitely the leader in this trio. Trix is a non-binary bird. This teaches kids about gender equality and how anyone is welcome in the world. They moved to Manhattan not too long ago with Cedar and their parents. Trix is a bright and passionate Otter-Cat hybrid who lets their bravery lead them. Trix and Cedar have been friends since they were little, however Benjamin was late to join the party. Trix met Benjamin as they were waiting for cedar to get ice cream and saw what they would describe as “a small feather ball sitting alone on a bench.” They were a little surprised at their apprehensiveness but got the impression that the city could be rather rough. After that night Trix and Benjamin started talking and became good friends. All around Trix is an amazing friend and a kind role model. Everyone small and large will want to be just like them.
